Chapter One Notes:


The detective finds himself interviewing a suspect who is currently residing in prision. He is accused of killing one of his clients, and is a tarot card seer. He claims he is innocent, and it is the power of the cards that killed them, but no one believes him.


Chapter Two Notes:


The detective goes to the funeral of the victim, and talks to the family about their history with the occult. They talked about the victim's research into vampires and witches, and how he would have strange dreams.


Chapter Three Notes:


The detective has a dream about the suspect sitting in a sea of star in heaven, and the three suns are shining brightly from every angle. One sun has a shadow of a fish, another of an arrow, and another of a cup. When he wakes up, he hears on the news that there are rumors of war.


Chapter Four Notes:


He goes to the orphanage and decides to finally adopt a child which he has been considering for a long time. He adopts twins, a girl and boy, and must have three meetings with them before he is allowed to finally let them live with him.
